About the Firm

Legal Excellence
Built in Abuja,
Felt Across Nigeria

Founded in 2010, Rens Attorney began with two visionary lawyers who left their previous engagements to build something different — a law firm where results, not reputation alone, define success. From a modest practice, we have grown into a full-service firm headquartered at 10 Optimum Hotel, Area 1, Garki, Abuja.

Today, we have established branches in Lagos, Port Harcourt, and Aberdeen, Scotland. Our reach is genuinely national: any Nigerian citizen across all 36 states can access our legal services remotely, without the burden of travelling to Abuja.

We attract top-level transactional and litigation work from individuals, corporations, and government bodies, guided by our collegiate culture, innovation, and a relentless commitment to results. We also proudly maintain pro bono services for qualifying indigent members of the public.
